Get To Know Us.

The Mission of Web3 Women in Science is to address the critical underrepresentation of womxn at the intersection of science and web3, sometimes referred to as "DeSci" for Decentralized Science.

We are a community with teeth. Our core tenets are: (1) serving as an efficient pipeline to onboad women into novel and existing DeSci organization and (2) serving as the web3 evolution of labor union, providing the support needed to keep women in this space once they arrive. In the future, holding our NFT will grant membership and voting rights, including meta-voting using community-owned governance tokens for DeSci DAOs.

Connect

We are an early stage organization but are busy at work. Our members and their efforts can be found throughout various online platforms such as Twitter, Discord, and Telegram. Our community is composed of powerful allies for female empowerment in the scientific community, which includes research scientists, science communicators (writers, illustrators, podcasters, etc.) biotech and industry professionals, healthcare workers, and science enthusiasts.

Twitter

Our organization posts updates and news to our official Twitter account. Here you can find updates about our organization's plans, meetings, and any upcoming events. We regularly host Twitter Spaces and many of our members also host DeSci focused spaces as well.

Discord

Connect with our Discord community where you'll find conversations centered about Web3, science, and our communities' involvement. You don't have to be a science professional or have any tech background to join in the conversation!
